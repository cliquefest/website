About Us


Clique is an Association of Computer Engineers run by Computer Science students of the Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science. [MITS]



MISSION

To build confidence in students by providing the highest quality technologies in computer science and to make
graduates with basic foundation so that they are enlightened with knowledge.
To upgrade students with innovative capabilities in the fast changing field of Computer science and Engineering.


VISION

Envolve students with latest skills and practical knowledge in core area to become well prepared computing professionals.
Providing an Environment in which students are given the essential resources to research and solve real-world problems.
